Why Lebron is the GOAT

Here is just a small list of his numerous accolades and achievements:

  • Longevity: James has performed at an All-NBA level throughout his career, and is still one of the league's best players in his age 40 season.
  • Record-breaking: James became the NBA's all-time leading scorer in 2023, and the first player to score over 40,000 career points during the 2023-24 season.
  • All-Star and All-NBA selections: James holds the record for most All-Star Game and All-NBA selections.
  • Championships: James has won four NBA championships with three different teams.
  • MVP awards: James has been named NBA MVP four times.
  • Playoffs wins: James leads the league in total playoffs wins.
  • Finals MVP awards: James is second in Finals MVP awards.
  • Impact on the game: Some say that James' impact on the game is felt on the court, and that he's surpassed all expectations placed on him.
